Welcome to GL BUDDY

GL BUDDY is an innovative chatbot designed specifically for GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management. Our platform serves as a comprehensive information hub for students, faculty, parents, and visitors, providing instant access to crucial information about the institution.

As your virtual assistant, GL BUDDY is available 24/7 to answer queries, provide guidance, and ensure that you have easy access to all the information you need about our institution.

Key Features

  • Instant access to academic information and schedules
  • Quick navigation to important college resources
  • Real-time updates about college events and notices
  • Easy access to examination schedules and results
  • Information about campus facilities and services
  • Direct links to important college portals

Our Mission

Our mission is to streamline communication and information access within the GL Bajaj community. We aim to provide accurate, timely, and relevant information to all stakeholders, enhancing the overall educational experience at GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management.

Why GL BUDDY?

  • User-friendly interface for seamless navigation
  • Comprehensive coverage of college-related information
